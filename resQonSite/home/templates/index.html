Process

How ResQon Works

Discover how our advanced drone system operates in fire emergency situations to maximize rescue efficiency.

01

Fire Detection & Drone Deployment

When a fire occurs, rescue teams deploy the ResQon drone which immediately begins scanning the building using floor plans for initial navigation.

02

AI-Based Human Detection

The fire-resistant drone enters the scene using thermal cameras and computer vision technology to detect survivors even through smoke and high-temperature environments.

03

Real-time Route Analysis

Once survivors are located, AI algorithms calculate the optimal rescue routes considering building structure and fire conditions, transmitting this data in real-time to rescue teams.

04

Rescue Operation Support

Rescue teams use ResQon's guidance to safely and quickly reach survivors, with the system continuously recalculating routes to adapt to changing fire conditions.

Use Cases

Application Areas

ResQon can be deployed in various environments and rescue scenarios where rapid response is critical.

Industrial Facilities

Large warehouses, factories, and power plants with complex layouts benefit from ResQon's advanced navigation capabilities and fire-resistant design.

Commercial Buildings

Shopping malls, high-rise buildings, and underground markets where large numbers of people gather and complex evacuation routes are needed.

Public Facilities

Hospitals, schools, subway stations and other public institutions requiring rapid evacuation assistance and precise locating of trapped individuals.

Natural Disasters

Forest fires, earthquakes and other natural disaster sites requiring survivor search and rescue operations in difficult-to-navigate environments.
